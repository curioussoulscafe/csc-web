Insights From Our Gatherings

Curious about our conversations? Here's a glimpse into the profound and thought-provoking topics we've explored together in our sessions across the globe.

Singapore / January 2025

On Authenticity, Values & Responsibility

The conversation moved from personal growth goals like authenticity and mindfulness to the complexities of personal responsibility, and challenging gender stereotypes in modern society.
Singapore / January 2025

On Love, Happiness & Success

Is love an action or a reaction? This question sparked a deep philosophical debate, exploring love as a conscious choice. Success was similarly defined not by comparison, but by self-growth and inner peace.
Kuala Lumpur / January 2025

On Self-Doubt & Facing Mortality

Participants identified self-doubt as a major obstacle, agreeing that "baby steps" and "showing up" are powerful first moves. The conversation also touched on facing mortality as a motivator to live more fully.
Singapore / January 2025

On Crafting a Legacy

If you could write your own obituary, what would it say? This led to a profound dialogue about mortality, values, and the impact of the small, meaningful moments that define a life well-lived.
Taipei / December 2024

On Addiction & Simple Pleasures

What's your thought on addiction? The group discussed how many addictions stem from underlying issues and the importance of mindfulness in finding happiness in simple, everyday moments.
Taipei / December 2024

On Imposter Syndrome, AI & Minimalism

From overcoming imposter syndrome with proactive strategies to the impact of AI on creativity, the group also explored the Taoist concept of "nothingness" and its connection to modern minimalism.
Hong Kong / August 2024

On Meaning, Habits & Generational Concerns

Participants explored life as a "process of becoming" and discussed personal struggles with bad habits, while also expressing concerns about the next generation's empathy and critical thinking skills.
Hong Kong / August 2024

On Group Dynamics & Personal Freedom

What conversation do you need to have to set yourself free? One participant argued that true liberation comes from reducing external influences, while another believed open conversation fosters collective freedom.
Hong Kong / August 2024

On Dealing with Pain & Rediscovering Dreams

When asked how to deal with pain, a participant shared a journey of managing chronic pain through mindfulness and acceptance, concluding, “Pain is the evidence that you are a living entity.”
Hong Kong / August 2024

On Suffering, Spirituality & Meaning

How to overcome suffering? The group explored finding strength through self-compassion and acceptance, with one participant noting, “You can’t expect unconditional love from others, but you can always love yourself unconditionally.”
Hong Kong / August 2024

On Choices, Regrets & Hindsight

If you could go back 10 years and make one change, what would it be? Participants reflected on embracing vulnerability, taking more risks, and the importance of making choices that align with their true selves.
Hong Kong / July 2024

On Happiness & Limiting Beliefs

Why is sorrow more unforgettable than happiness? Participants explored how to redefine happiness by shifting focus from what we lack to appreciating what we already have.
