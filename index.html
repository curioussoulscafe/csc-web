What is Curious Souls Café?

Founded to counter the social bubbles and isolation of modern life, we believe in the power of face-to-face conversations to cultivate deeper bonds, genuine friendships, and personal growth.

Beyond Small Talk

We focus on deep, introspective conversations that go beyond superficial topics, creating an outlet to explore thoughts and feelings often avoided elsewhere.

Safe & Non-Judgmental

Our events are safe spaces where you can share thoughts and vulnerabilities without fear of judgment, encouraging honesty and authenticity.

Fostering Connection

We provide a platform for strangers from diverse backgrounds to connect on a deeper level, bridging gaps that might not be crossed in daily life.

How Our Events Work

Our public events balance structure and spontaneity to create a comfortable and engaging atmosphere for everyone.

Round One: Curated Questions

We begin with thoughtfully curated question cards to spark reflection and help participants build rapport.

Round Two: Your Questions

An optional second round invites you to write and share your own questions, allowing for deeper, more organic conversations driven by the group.

Moment of Truth

We conclude with a 5-10 minute reflection session where everyone can share key takeaways, insights, or lessons learned from the discussion.

Insights From Our Gatherings

Curious about our conversations? Here's a glimpse into the profound and thought-provoking topics we explore together.

Hong Kong / July 2024

On Happiness & Limiting Beliefs

Why is sorrow more unforgettable than happiness? Participants explored how to redefine happiness by shifting focus from what we lack to appreciating what we already have.
Singapore / January 2025

On Crafting a Legacy

If you could write your own obituary, what would it say? This led to a profound dialogue about mortality, values, and the impact of the small, meaningful moments that define a life well-lived.
Taipei / December 2024

On Addiction & Simple Pleasures

What's your thought on addiction? The group discussed how many addictions stem from underlying issues and the importance of mindfulness in finding happiness in simple, everyday moments.
